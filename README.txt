KEYLOGGER PROJECT WITH AUTOMATIC GMAIL SERVICE MESSENGING

Send reports to Your inbox after specified amount of time to a specified inbox.

For the project to work put Your email address and password into enviroment variables on Your PC.
Where:
  EMAIL_ADDRESS - is Your Gmail address
  EMAIL_PASSWORD - is Your password to that mail inbox
and launch the code

It will send emails with all the alphanumeric keystrokes values and some of the special keys logged in plaintext
